About me

Gamer with a Dream

Founder and CEO Nexal Gaming Community, Esports Consultant, Manager and Brand Influencer. I started my Esports Journey 2019 immediately after my high school graduation, my goal is develop Esports in Nigeria and as well as Africa. I started the Nigerian Gaming Community now Nexal Gaming Community.

The problem I noticed within the esports in Nigeria was lack of data which led to lack of information and untimately lack of communication. It was a free for all environment with really no direction. Esports has been around in Nigeria to the best of my knowledge since 2008/2009 but it wasn't until 2019 esports began real development in Nigeria and I'm certain i palyed a role in that development. You see when i started the NGC, the plan was to be the middle man connecting the gamers to the tournament Organisers making it easier for people to access information regarding thier favorite games in Nigeria.

I still carry out that plan even till today with Nexal Gaming Community but on a much larger scale and with teams of content creators, content writers, graphic designers, web designers, tournament managers and of course gamers.
